Poem 06 - I Love You




I love you.
And you will never hear me say it.
How come you are bigger than my heart.
And you perfectly fit.
You think you have a glimpse of who I am.
Until the phone rang.
I didn't want to tell you my story.
As I said.
I don't want to tell you every time I talk to you.
The time always overran.
And my duty is to tell you via mail or a telegram.
And of course, the phone rang.
If you believe others they will tell you I am a scam.
But I am just a simple man.
Who has to tell you I am not who you think again.
You asked me when I will see you again?
I replied never is the when.
I still like you and it overwhelms.
It overwhelms my heart then.
I couldn't tell you who I am.
I will leave you as a fulfilled man.
The train will leave at ten.
And of course, at this time I went.
And I am appreciating this last moment I spend.
We will never meet, sure I am.
But I will tell you goodbye.
May we meet haphazardly again.
To tell you, I am a spy.
And you were just a mission in the plan.
But I loved you over time.
Even I didn't have the right to do it back then.
Goodbye, my love.
