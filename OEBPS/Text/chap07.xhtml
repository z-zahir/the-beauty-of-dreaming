Poem 07 - Justice or Not




I don't want to fight.
I don't want people who did that either pay.
The people who stole from me my day.
On this day I could be bright.
But I am just a junk today.
Because the light is gray.
Only misery and depression stay.
I always doubted the existence of God.
The one I know did only betray.
I don't know if one day.
I will find myself anyway.
I am still angry about my destiny.
It's like losing a part of me as my identity.
Once upon a time like yesterday.
It was from drugs to ecstasy.
Today it's like I use chemotherapy.
To heal the part that is still damaged mentally.
My brain got disturbed chemically.
And I am telling my story.
To entertain essentially.
But I am suffering terribly.
I will die alone and lonely.
As I have been my entire life, me only with me.
You might read this carelessly.
My voice will pass from primary to secondary.
From secondary to nobody.
From nobody breathlessly.
From breathlessly to words printed emotionally.
From words to decades or even centuries.
Staying in one place and finding you finally.
I will tell you one thing.
No matter who you are, you will suffer blindly.
But the more you stand up the more you carry.
Carry the power in you to fight confidently.
This was my message now take it carefully.
It was from a damaged soul killed potentially.
A soul that has only the ink to spray in paper with all intimacy.
