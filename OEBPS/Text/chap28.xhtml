Rhyme 28 - The Young Guy I Was




I don't know how I make friends. I just do.
I don't know where my father's money comes from. I just take them.
I don't know what is the music I am listening to means. I just listen to them.
I don't know what is the goal of the movies I am watching. I just watch them.
I don't know why I study and why I bother doing it. I just do it.
I don't know why adults have so many regrets. I just don't care.
I don't know what is in the mind of the girl I love. I wish I do.
I don't know why I want to be popular. I just do.
I don't know why belonging makes me feel invincible. It just does.
