Poem 13 - Teenage Secret Lover




Since I saw you.
My heart found it new.
I have to find the path through.
Through the feelings that are screwed.
I loved you secretly.
And you are still in my heart with some view.
My heart grows increasingly.
And my feelings still belong to you.
Do you remember the friend that loved you?
It was me and I was with my heart so cruel.
This friend texted you.
You loved it.
And I didn't know what to do.
With you I am shy, I admit.
I am the guy that I don't know.
But you are the girl that I knew.
You told me this guy is among riches, but who?
Rich with feeling with a big heart and he did it.
He gained my heart better than you.
You know him is it true?
Yes, I know him, but what will you do?
