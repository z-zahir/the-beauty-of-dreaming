Story 03 - Am I a Physicist




"Teacher? How can I know if I am a physicist or not?"
The teacher replied, "Have you read Alice in the wonderland?"
Then the student answered, "Yeah, and it's a good one, but why?"
The teacher, "What do you think of Alice falling in the well, part of the story?"
The student, "Well... I think it's just normal!"
The teacher, "Nothing special on this part of the story?"
The student, "I liked how Lewis Carroll described somehow the time as motion of the fall."
The teacher, "I think you are already a good physicist. Keep up the good work, and Good luck."
